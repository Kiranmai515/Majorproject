Balanced Diet for Age Group 10-15

Nutrient Requirements

  • Carbohydrates: 45-65% of total daily calories
  • Proteins: 10-30% of total daily calories
  • Fats: 25-35% of total daily calories
  • Vitamins and Minerals: Essential vitamins like A, B (especially B12 and folate), C, D, E, and K, and minerals like calcium, iron, magnesium, and zinc.

Daily Caloric Intake

  • Boys: 1,500 to 2,600 calories
  • Girls: 1,400 to 2,200 calories

Food Groups and Recommended Daily Servings

Fruits

1.5 to 2 cups

Vegetables

2 to 3 cups

Grains

5 to 7 ounces

Protein Foods

5 to 6.5 ounces

Dairy

3 cups

Nutrient-Rich Foods

Carbohydrates

  • Whole Grains: Brown rice, whole wheat bread, quinoa, oats
  • Fruits: Apples, bananas, berries
  • Vegetables: Potatoes, corn, peas

Proteins

  • Lean Meats: Chicken, turkey, lean beef
  • Fish: Salmon, tuna, mackerel
  • Legumes: Lentils, chickpeas, black beans
  • Nuts and Seeds: Almonds, walnuts, chia seeds
  • Dairy: Milk, cheese, yogurt

Fats

  • Healthy Oils: Olive oil, canola oil
  • Nuts and Seeds: Flaxseeds, sunflower seeds, walnuts
  • Avocados
  • Fatty Fish: Salmon, trout

Sample Balanced Diet Plan

Breakfast

  • Whole grain cereal with milk
  • Fruit: Banana or apple
  • Egg or yogurt

Mid-Morning Snack

  • Nuts or seeds
  • Fruit: Apple slices or orange

Lunch

  • Whole grain sandwich with lean meat and veggies
  • Vegetable soup or salad
  • Fruit: Grapes or a small apple

Afternoon Snack

  • Yogurt or cheese sticks
  • Carrot sticks or celery
